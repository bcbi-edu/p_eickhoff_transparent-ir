TERMS AND PRIVACY

This experiment investigates the effects of providing transparency and explainability of information retrieval systems to users.

In this experiment, you will be given a dataset on a specific topic and a search engine to help look for documents. You will also be given an exploration task to guide your search.

At the end of the experiment, you will fill out a survey describing your experience with the search engine.

The experiment is a Brown University research study that will take place over a single session of at most thirty minutes and will take place at your current location.
